# Mall Explorer Brand Guide

## Brand Name
**Mall Explorer** - Your guide to discovering malls, events, shops, and deals

## Tagline
"Explore. Navigate. Discover."

## Logo
- Primary logo: `/images/mall-explorer-logo.jpg`
- Icon: Folded map icon in mint green (#00D9B1) on dark navy background
- The logo represents navigation and exploration

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Navy Blue**: #0F172A (Primary brand color, backgrounds, headers)
- **Mint Green**: #00D9B1 (Accent color, CTAs, navigation elements)

### Secondary Colors
- **Light Background**: #F8FAFC (Page backgrounds, cards)
- **Dark Text**: #1E293B (Body text)
- **Muted Text**: #64748B (Secondary text, captions)

### Accent Colors
- **Teal Ring**: #134E4A (Logo outer ring)
- **Success Green**: #10B981 (Live status indicators)
- **Warning Amber**: #F59E0B (Coming soon badges)

## Typography
- **Headings**: System font stack (SF Pro, Segoe UI, etc.) - Bold weights
- **Body**: System font stack - Regular weight
- **Accent**: Monospace for data/metrics

## Brand Voice
- Professional yet approachable
- Data-driven and insightful
- Focused on value for both shoppers and mall operators

## Key Messages

### For Shoppers (B2C)
- "Find the shop you're looking for 10x faster"
- "Discover events, deals, and promotions"
- "Indoor navigation without getting lost"

### For Mall Operators (B2B)
- "Capture shopper intent, not just footfall"
- "Zero beacons, zero hardware — just insights"
- "Know what shoppers are looking for, not just how many came"

## Value Proposition Comparison

| Traditional Footfall Tech | Mall Explorer |
|---------------------------|---------------|
| Counts bodies | Captures **intent** |
| Knows how many entered | Knows **where they wanted to go** |
| Anonymous | Can build **opted-in profiles** |
| Hardware cost + maintenance | **Zero infrastructure** |
| No engagement channel | **Direct promo channel** to shoppers |

## Monthly Demand Report Metrics
- Top 10 searched stores
- Top 5 searched categories
- Unmet demand signals (e.g., "34 searches for Nike — no Nike store")
- Promo performance (impressions → clicks → navigations)
- Busiest navigation days
- Most common entry points
